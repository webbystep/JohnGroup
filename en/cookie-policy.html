Cookie policy

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file that a website places on the visitor's device (computer, smartphone, tablet) through the browser. Cookies help the website remember the visitor's preferences, make navigation easier, and collect anonymous statistical data to improve the user experience.

Types of cookies we use

The following cookie categories are used on this website:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: essential for the operation of the website (e.g. session identifier, security settings). These cookies do not require consent.
  • Functional cookies: improve the user experience (e.g. language settings, preferences).
  • Statistical cookies: collect anonymized data about website usage, used to improve user experience and content.
  • Marketing cookies: enable personalized advertising and the measurement of advertising campaigns. These are placed only with the visitor's explicit consent.
Third-party cookies

The website may use third-party services (e.g. Google Fonts, Google Analytics, social media embeds). These providers may place their own cookies on your device, subject to their own privacy and cookie policies.

Managing cookies

You can accept, reject, or delete previously placed cookies through your browser settings. In most browsers, these options are available under Settings / Privacy.

Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may result in some website functions being unavailable or only partially available.

Withdrawing consent

You may withdraw previously given cookie consent at any time by clearing your browser's cache and cookies, or by reopening the cookie settings panel on the website.

More information

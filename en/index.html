How can we help?

CNC milling

On 3- and 5-axis machining centres, up to 2200×2250×800 mm, we produce one-offs and small to medium series from steel, non-ferrous metals, aluminium and plastics — including raw-material sourcing, surface treatment, painting or grinding on request.

CNC turning

Up to 400×1500 mm, we machine rotational parts with live tooling and short lead times — with surface treatment and cylindrical grinding available on request.

Complex machining

Turnkey solutions for fabricated structures — from welding and bending to complete frames, tables and other assemblies, with powder coating or high-gloss finishing when needed.
